Agile Software Development ()
Definition: An Agile Software Development Driver is a motivating factor that promotes the adoption of agile methodologies to enhance flexibility, speed, and collaboration in software development. It aims to deliver high-quality software incrementally, respond quickly to changes, and meet customer needs effectively through iterative development and continuous feedback.
